----------------------------------------------------
  WHISPER MODEL SIZES
----------------------------------------------------

  tiny   = fastest, lowest quality (1GB RAM)
  base   = default, good balance   (1GB RAM)
  small  = better quality          (2GB RAM)
  medium = high quality, slow      (5GB RAM)
  large  = best quality, very slow (10GB RAM)
